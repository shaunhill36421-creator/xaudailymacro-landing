Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-03

This Privacy Policy describes how XAU Daily Macro ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information from visitors to our landing page and customers of our Telegram bot product (XAU Daily Macro Pro).

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase our product through a marketplace platform (WarriorPlus, JVZoo, or similar), we receive the following from the platform:

  • Your first name and email address (as you provided to the marketplace at checkout)
  • Transaction identifier, purchase amount, and timestamp
  • Refund status, if applicable

When you interact with our Telegram bot (@CryptoForexAnalyzebot), Telegram provides us:

  • Your Telegram user ID (numeric identifier)
  • Your chosen language preference (English or Vietnamese)
  • Commands you send to the bot (e.g., /analyze XAUUSD) for the purpose of returning a response

We do not collect: your phone number, your physical address, your real name beyond what the marketplace provides, your financial account details, your trading history outside the bot, or any biometric data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To grant and maintain your 30-day Pro access tied to your Telegram account
  • To respond to your bot commands and deliver analysis
  • To send transactional emails (purchase confirmation, renewal reminder on Day 25, refund confirmation)
  • To provide customer support if you contact us
  • To comply with legal obligations and marketplace platform requirements

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Customer records (Telegram ID, email from marketplace, plan status, period end date) are stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database with row-level security. Access is restricted to the product owner and authorized service accounts. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third-party marketers.

4. Third-Party Services We Use

  • WarriorPlus & JVZoo: payment processing and marketplace listing. Each has its own privacy policy that governs your checkout data.
  • PayPal: payment processor for marketplace transactions.
  • Telegram: bot delivery channel. Your interaction with the bot is governed by Telegram's privacy policy.
  • Twelve Data & Yahoo Finance: market data providers used to generate analysis. Your queries do not include any personal identifier.
  • Supabase: database hosting (located in the United States).

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are a resident of the European Union or California, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email alexnguyen2304@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account record for as long as your Pro access is active plus 12 months for refund and accounting purposes. After this period, your data is anonymized or deleted. Marketplace transaction records may be retained by the marketplace platforms according to their own retention policies.

7. Children

This product is not directed to anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Cookies and Tracking

This landing page does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking pixels. The marketplace checkout pages (WarriorPlus, JVZoo) may use their own cookies — refer to their policies.
